<h1 align="center">PharmaFlow Backend</h1>

Welcome to the official backend repository for PharmaFlow, an intelligent e-commerce platform for online pharmacies. This project is developed within the PharmaFlow UNSA organization at the University of Sarajevo, Faculty of Electrical Engineering.

_**Project Overview**_

PharmaFlow is a sophisticated web application designed to digitalize and enhance the pharmaceutical shopping experience. Beyond standard e-commerce features, the platform integrates intelligent medical rules, safety checks, and personalized health management tools to ensure safe and efficient access to medications.

_**Architecture**_

The backend is built using a Microservices Architecture to ensure modularity, scalability, and easier maintenance. The system is divided into five core services:

- **User & Health Service** Manages user accounts, family sub-profiles (Family Hub), and detailed health records including allergies and active therapies.

- **Product & Medical Service** Maintains the central pharmaceutical catalog, active substances, drug interactions, and contraindication rules.

- **Pharmacy & Inventory Service** Tracks real-time stock levels across various pharmacy locations and manages order fulfillment logistics.

- **Order & Prescription Service** Handles the complete checkout flow, including secure prescription uploads, validation, and payment processing.

- **Smart Features Service** Powers intelligent functionalities such as symptom-based search, AI recommendations, and automated refill algorithms.
